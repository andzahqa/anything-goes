### Cafe Operations

**Vietnamese Drip Coffee Preparation**:
```
Equipment: Phin (metal filter)
Process:
1. Grind coffee (medium-coarse)
2. Place in phin over cup
3. Pour small amount hot water, let bloom (30 sec)
4. Add full water, let drip (4-5 minutes)
5. Add condensed milk (ca phe sua)
6. Mix and serve over ice

Time: 5-7 minutes per coffee
Capacity: 8-12 coffees/hour per phin
Multiple phins: 4-6 running simultaneously
```

**Cafe Service Flow**:
```
Customer Enters:
1. Choose seat (inside or outside)
2. Waiter approaches
3. Order verbally (menu optional, regulars know)
4. Coffee prepared
5. Served with glass of tea (free)
6. Customer sits 1-3 hours (normal!)
7. Order more or leave
8. Pay when leaving (honor system mostly)

Table Turnover: 3-5 per day (very low!)
But: High margin on coffee (70%) makes up for it
```
